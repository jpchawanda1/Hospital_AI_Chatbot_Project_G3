Hospital AI Agent System: Intelligent Medical Information Assistant
NLP-Enhanced AI System for Nairobi Hospital & Kenyatta National Hospital with Advanced ML, Semantic Understanding & Reinforcement Learning
AI Term Project Group 3 • jpchawanda1 • Healthcare Information Systems • August 2025
Introduction & Problem Statement
Healthcare Challenge: Patients struggle to access accurate medical information about hospital services, pricing, appointments, and emergency contacts for Nairobi's major hospitals, leading to delayed care and confusion.

Solution: Develop an AI-powered Hospital Information Assistant that provides intelligent, 24/7 access to comprehensive medical information for Nairobi Hospital and Kenyatta National Hospital.

Project Objectives:

  • Create comprehensive medical knowledge base (1000+ Q&A pairs)
  • Implement advanced NLP with Sentence Transformers
  • Deploy machine learning for intelligent query matching
  • Build reinforcement learning for continuous improvement
  • Provide real hospital information (contacts, pricing, services)

Healthcare Impact:

  • Accessibility: 24/7 hospital information access
  • Efficiency: Reduced wait times for basic inquiries
  • Accuracy: Verified medical information
  • Coverage: Two major Nairobi hospitals
